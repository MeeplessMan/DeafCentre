About Us

The DUT Booking System is here to simplify the appointment process between students and lecturers. Our goal is to ensure that students get the support they need by providing a platform for easy, organized communication and scheduling.

Our Impact

Over 500 students and lecturers use the DUT Booking System to streamline their academic meetings and ensure that communication is efficient and effective. We continue to enhance the platform to meet growing demands and challenges.
